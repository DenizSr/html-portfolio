The Best Movies

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Howl's Moving Castle

We all love Miyazaki. But to be honest this is my favorite. Amazingly beautiful images and a very heartwarming story.

Pride and Prejudice

Jane Austen's lovely book on screen. Super romantic imo. (Be honest; how many of you cried watching this scene?)

Lord of The Rings/The Fellowship of The Ring

When someone says fantasy i think Tolkien. Love the book. Love the movie. Highly recommend.
